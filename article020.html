Peanut Butter Fudge Protein Cookie

Christina Hallalulu
2014-11-03
A peanut butter spin on regular chocolate-chip cookies, these moist and delicious Peanut butter fudge chocolate chunk protein cookies are sensational.

After the success of my last Chocolate Chip Protein Cookie recipe, I decided to try making them again. This time, with peanut butter. (If you like peanut butter, you're going to love these!) Soft, moist peanut butter fudge with chocolate chunks, all rolled into a healthy, protein-packed cookie. All it takes is 5 ingredients and a craving for peanut butter to make this delicious, Lila, Chop! recipe for beginners.

Ingredients
amount name
scoop Myprotein Micellar Casein protein powder, vanilla , or
scoop Any Vanilla or Peanut-butter flavoured, Casein or Whey protein powder
tbsp P28 High Protein Peanut Butter spread, white chocolate, or
tbsp any peanut butter
tbsp Sweet Freedom natural sweetener dark syrup, or
tbsp honey or agave syrup
tbsp Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chunks
tbsp almond milk
Servings: (Total Calories: .)

As you can see, the recipe works by using the peanut butter mix combined with casein protein powder to substitute flour, eggs, and butter, and the fruit-concentrate syrup instead of sugar. That's how you make a simple, clean, gluten/lactose/sugar-free cookie that's high in protein and still wonderfully tasty.

Steps
time instruction
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.
Roll into 4 balls and place on a baking paper-lined oven tray.
10-15 minutes Bake at 160'C (320'F) and check after 10 minutes to make sure they don't overcook.
20 min Devour!

Product Review

Once upon a time, consumers had only two options to choose between when it came to peanut butter: chunky or creamy. Today, there are so many different types of peanut butters on the market, ranging from 100% organic ground peanuts to reduced-fat, no-stir, flavoured peanut butter with marshmallow bits inside. The peanut butter I used in my recipe was the first one I have seen that's protein-fortified with whey protein isolate. And let me tell you, it was yummy! Introducing the white chocolate-flavoured high protein Peanut Butter spread, from P28 Foods . With 14g of protein per 2 tbsp-serving, this is a highly nutritious spread!
